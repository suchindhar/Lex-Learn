Article 51A | Contains all eleven Fundamental Duties
42nd Amendment | Added Fundamental Duties to the Constitution in 1976
86th Amendment | Added the duty to provide education to children (Article 51A(k))
Article 51A(a) | Respect Constitution, National Flag, National Anthem
Article 51A(b) | Uphold sovereignty, unity, and integrity of India
Article 51A(c) | Defend the country and render national service
Article 51A(d) | Promote harmony and common brotherhood
Article 51A(e) | Renounce practices derogatory to women's dignity
Article 51A(f) | Preserve rich heritage of Indian culture
Article 51A(g) | Protect environment, forests, wildlife, and have compassion for living creatures
Article 51A(h) | Develop scientific temper, humanism, and spirit of inquiry
Article 51A(i) | Safeguard public property and abjure violence
Article 51A(j) | Strive for excellence in all spheres of activity
Article 51A(k) | Duty of parents to provide education for children aged 6-14
Non-justiciable | Fundamental Duties cannot be enforced directly by courts
